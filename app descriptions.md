## App Store Description (4,000 characters max)

**Donna — AI Assistant for Aging with Grace**

Your parents deserve more than silence between visits. Donna is an AI-powered voice assistant that calls your loved ones every day to offer warm conversation, gentle reminders, and meaningful connection — so they stay independent and you get peace of mind.

**How It Works**

Donna makes friendly phone calls to your loved one at the times you choose. No app install, no complicated setup for them — just a phone call from a warm, familiar voice. Donna remembers past conversations, learns their interests, and gets more personal over time. She naturally weaves reminders and daily tasks into conversation so nothing important slips through the cracks.

After every call, you receive a summary of conversation, activity, and wellbeing highlights right in the app — so you always know how they're doing, even from miles away.

**Your Caregiving Command Center**

The Donna app puts you in control of your loved one's care:

- Dashboard: See at a glance when the next call is, read recent call summaries, and monitor engagement trends. Get the peace of mind of knowing how your loved one is doing until you're able to next check in yourself.

- Reminders: Add, edit, and manage everything Donna should remind your loved one about — medications, appointments, watering the garden, calling the doctor. Set reminders as daily, weekly, or one-time. Donna delivers them naturally in conversation, not as robotic alerts.

- Schedule: Choose which days and times Donna calls using an intuitive calendar view. Adjust anytime as routines change. Add multiple calls per day if needed.

- Instant Check-In: Worried about Mom but can't call that instant? Tap one button and Donna calls her right now for a friendly check-in. Add optional context notes so Donna knows what to ask about.

- Alerts: If Donna hears anything troublesome during a call, you're notified right away. Full call summaries are always available in the app.

- Loved One Profile: Update their interests, location, and preferences anytime. The more Donna knows, the better the conversations become. Track what topics light them up.

**Why Families Choose Donna**

Loneliness among seniors is a public health crisis — as harmful as smoking 15 cigarettes a day. Between your visits, there are long hours of silence. Donna fills those gaps with warm, engaging conversation that builds real connection over time.

Unlike medical alert systems that only react to emergencies, Donna proactively improves your loved one's daily life by addressing isolation, forgetfulness, and cognitive engagement — every single day.

- Calls feel natural, not robotic — powered by advanced conversational AI
- Remembers their stories, interests, and preferences across every call
- Delivers reminders naturally woven into conversation
- Detects concerns and alerts you when something feels off
- Simple 3-minute setup — no tech skills required for your loved one
- No contracts, cancel anytime
